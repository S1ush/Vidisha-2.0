About Us

ViDISHA means ?

Vidisha the word means multiple directions signifying various aspects of life.

Introduction

As the name speaks itself vidisha hosts various competitions in various fields like singing, dancing, drama and much more conveying all the cultural branches. The intent of choosing this name was to provide a stage to raw talent and building aspirations Started in 2014, as an indoor event and over the years has achieved the intercollege status.

What We Do?

We at Vishwaniketan, not only provide students with academic excellence but also provide a boost to extra-curricular activities. With a rich cultural history, our team of vidisha fully prepared to give our best to provide everyone with an opportunity to find out the fun side in each and every event and promise to give you all a memorizing experience.

ViDISHA is the cultural fest of Vishwaniketan's iMEET
